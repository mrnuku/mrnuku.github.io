About me

Started programming by myself (self taught) almost after i got my first computer at the age of 17.
First i was very interested of video games as a young boy... this was the era of the Quake 3 games, so my first experiences was in the C language, made a lot of mods/optimization for those games.

I love linux if its either Gentoo, Alpine or Ubuntu/Debian.
I work on a mac, proud owner of an M1 apple silicon.
